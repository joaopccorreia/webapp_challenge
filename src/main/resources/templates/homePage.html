Sell your designer bags for Farfetch credit

Introducing Farfetch Second Life, a new service to enable you to make space in your wardrobe by selling your bags in exchange for credits to spend on Farfetch. Give your bags a second lease of life. Earn Credit.*

Available in the UK, European Union and Norway. Please see FAQs for more details. Based elsewhere? Get notified when Second Life launches near you.

Here's how it works

Get your quote

Upload photos of the bag you want to sell from the brands listed below, and within 2 business days we'll tell you how much credit you could earn

Arrange a free collection

Schedule a pick-up at a time that suits you, then we'll take care of the rest

Earn Farfetch credit

Get credit in just two business days once your bag has been received and verified at our warehouse

What's in it for me?

Instant payment

We give you credit as soon as your bag has been verified so you can fund your next purchase faster

Get something new

Use your credit to buy new things on Farfetch

No fees

The price we give you is the value of the credit you will get. No hidden fees.

Do good

By selling your bag, you're doing your bit to extend its life and help the environment
